Mouda an SouSou

We are two designers that have a passion for music, photography and most importantly, clothing! Mouda particularly likes high fashion, minimalism and metal music. SouSou enjoys gaming, and especially likes to go out and contemplate nature.

Our Goal

Provide quality pieces with unique sillouettes and textures at affordable prices without compromising on material or service.

What Inspires Us

We like art and artistic expression. We think that clothing is a big part of how people express their taste and personality. We find beauty in the unique style of each individual and we wanted to make more niche and expressive pieces more accessible.

How We Started

OUR BRAND IS STARTING RIGHT AS WE SPEAK! Feel free to leave us some feedback in the support section.
